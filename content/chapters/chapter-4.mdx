The world is "hyperbitcoinized". At least that's what they taught you in history class.
You're not totally sure what that means. 
Probably has something to do with people using bitcoin? But isn't that how it's always been?

Today is a big day though. 
There will be no more block subsidy. 
Transaction fees are all that's left to pay for security.
Are they enough? The boss has tasked you to figure this out.
The company might depend on this answer. Let's dive in.
